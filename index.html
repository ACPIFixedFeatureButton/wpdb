about

wpdb is a community-maintained archive of Windows Phone firmware files and resources for OEM devices that are not from Nokia/Microsoft.

Our mission is to preserve the legacy of Windows Phone by providing easy access to essential files for device recovery.

This project is not affiliated with Microsoft or any device manufacturers. All firmware files are the property of their respective owners.
